FeedMe

What's going on here?

FeedMe is a service aimed at bettering your health, and increasing the sustainability in your local communities. It's easy to use and costs absolutely nothing.

Start off by selecting some of your favourite foods, and we'll recommend recipies based on that! Our algorithms filter through thousands of recipes to give you something healthy, tasty, and suited to your likes.

Save your favourites and look for tags! We'll tell you when a meal contains seasonal, locally grown products. When this happens, FeedMe will even tell you where to buy it.

What are you waiting for? Get started today and start living your best life.
