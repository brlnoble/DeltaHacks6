FeedMe

What's going on here?

FeedMe is a service aimed at bettering your health, and increasing the sustainability in your local communities. It's easy to use and costs absolutely nothing.

The best food is fresh and homegrown. Our goal is to get you eating these local goodies, leaving you happier and healthier.

We'll tell you what is seasonally available, and suggest some recipes to go along with it When this happens, FeedMe will even tell you where to buy it.
